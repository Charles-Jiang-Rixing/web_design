Introduction

Nowadays, chronic diseases, including obesity, diabetes, cardiovascular disease, and cancer, are becoming increasingly prevalent. As people pay more attention to their health, preventive measures are taken. Daily diet now gains growing interest from the general public for its potential to prevent and improve these diseases. As many believe, “Food is medicine.”

Olive oil, a major component in Mediterranean diets, is one of the most recommended ingredients for daily consumption because of its various nutrients and health benefits.
