Health Benefits of Olive Oil

1. Heart Disease

Substantial evidence indicates that olive oil protects against heart disease and stroke and enhances heart health, which can be explained by less inflammation, lower blood pressure, and reduced low-density lipoprotein oxidation.

2. Brain Function

Olive oil can preserve brain function by improving oxidative stress, which is associated with cognitive disorders such as Alzheimer’s disease. Some Mediterranean diet studies have revealed that habitual consumption of olive oil reduces the risk of dementia and cognitive impairment.

3. Cancer

One significant factor for cancer is free radicals, while antioxidants in olive oil can effectively reduce the oxidative damage they cause. Therefore, olive oil has been shown to slow the growth and spread of cancer cells, including breast and digestive cancer.
