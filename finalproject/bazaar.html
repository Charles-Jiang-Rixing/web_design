Charity Bazaars

Last summer, I participated in a charity bazaar held by a charitable organization in Shanghai, China. There, we sold adorable toys, delicate gadgets, and cloth bags. Finally, the profit we gained was all donated to the program supporting needy families in remote areas.

This year, during the summer vacation, I launched a charity sale myself and set up a booth at the city center. Together with several friends, I sold self-made perfumes, key chains, postcards, etc. We ultimately gained a profit of over 200 dollars and then donated to a local charity organization.
