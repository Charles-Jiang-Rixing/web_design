Dream Home Teaching

This year, I spent four months teaching students from an underprivileged area in Northwestern China. During the process, they also taught me something invaluable: resilience and persistence. Eventually, to my relief, their grades were all improved significantly in the final exam.

Some of the students were about my age. Besides study, I also helped them cook meals and chatted with them every day. Therefore, we were more like friends instead of the teacher and students. This was indeed an unforgettable experience.
