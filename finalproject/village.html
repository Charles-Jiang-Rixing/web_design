Village Field Research

Last summer vacation, I conducted a one-week field research with other classmates in Nuanshui village, Gansu Province, China. There, I interviewed several villagers about their living conditions and witnessed some environmental pollution problems.

Finally, I co-authored and published an investigative article about the local water pollution issue due to fertilizer misuse and provided some solutions. I also created an online encyclopedia entry for the village to gain it more public attention.
